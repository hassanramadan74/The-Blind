We're Constant Contact. This is Our Story

Everything we do is rooted in helping the blind. Technology plays an increasingly important role in more and more people’s lives, on and off the field of play. It is central to every culture and society and is core to our health and happiness.

  • The blind do not settle for average. And neither do we. We have a clear mission: To be the best blind shoe brand in the world.
  • Every day, we come to work to create and sell the best Blind products in the world
  • We will always strive to expand the limits of human possibilities

We come to offer the best service and consumer experience – and to do it all in a sustainable way. We are the best when we are the credible, inclusive, and sustainable leader in our industry.
